Thursday, February 05, 2004  


Clwb Malu Cachu website back up
I’m pleased to announce that the Clwb Malu Cachu website, is back up and running again after a short intermission.

New on CMC
Chat! There is now a java chat room where you can go to talk to other Welsh learners and practice your Welsh. Use it well and use it often!

News! There’s a new CMC news blog that we will now be using instead of the front page to keep you up to date with what’s going on. Visit regularly, or subscribe to the RSS feed and download the news into your aggregator.

Give us cash! CMC is not financially supported by anyone. Suw pays for the hosting out of her own pocket. If you find the CMC site, please consider donating even a small amount to help keep CMC online.

Better still! If you are serious about learning Welsh, buy one of the Get Fluent CD-ROMs. The Get Fluent worksheets are perfect for learners of all levels, giving you bite-sized morsels of fun learning material to help you really get to grips with Welsh. Download a free worksheet and see for yourself! Plus, order the full course of 40 worksheets and get a free t-shirt. Go on - it’ll be the best thing you’ve done all day.

New CMC update nearly finished
