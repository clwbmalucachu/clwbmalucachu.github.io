bod - to be: future and future perfect tenses

Use with 'yn' for future tense: 	 "I will be washing" or "I will wash"
				"Will you be washing?" 
				"I won't be washing" or "I won't wash"

Use with 'wedi' for future perfect tense: 	"I will have washed" 
					"Will I have washed?"
					"I won't have washed"

bydda i
byddi di
bydd e
bydd hi
byddwn ni
byddwch chi
byddan nhw
