the future with gwneud

You can use the inflected future form of gwneud to make a future of any other verbnoun, by adding that verbnoun onto the end of gwneud, e.g. na i ffonio - I will phone. This construction is used to imply intention, and is an alternative to putting a preterite ending on to the verbnoun, e.g. na i ffonio - ffonia i - i'll phone.

Nei di and newch chi also function as a polite request, 'will you...?'.
