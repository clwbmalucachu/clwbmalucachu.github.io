imperfect/pluperfect

Use with 'yn' for Imperfect tense: 	"I was washing" (not "I did wash" nor "I washed")
					"Were you washing?" 
					"I wasn't washing" 

Use with 'wedi' for Pluperfect tense: 	"I had washed" 
					"Had I washed?"
					"I hadn't washed"

Note: These tenses have a written form, and a spoken form (which is an abbreviated version of the written form). It is best to learn both.
