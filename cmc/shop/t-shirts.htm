the cmc range of clothing!

A range of high-quality T-shirts, sweatshirts and hoodies with a choice of Welsh language slogans. There are styles for both men and women and a choice of colours. All items are made from high-quality, heavyweight cotton or poly-cotton which is long-lasting and comfortable to wear.

To buy online, please visit our shop on our sister-site, Get Fluent, but please do read this information first.

IMPORTANT NOTE: We take credit card fraud very seriously indeed and all transactions that go through our shop are checked vigorously by our payment service provider, Secure Trading. Please ensure that when you enter your invoice address it is the same one as on your credit/debit card statement, as it will be checked when the payment is processed. We also require you to enter the security code number - the last three digits on your card's signature strip. Any transactions that do not use the correct security code will be thoroughly investigated before approval.

Because we're a small business, we don't always have every t-shirt in stock. If we can't get your t-shirt printed within a reasonable time, we will contact you by email or phone and offer alternatives. We will NOT charge your credit card until your order has shipped.

ordering by traditional mail
We accept cheques and postal orders, as well as credit card details, by mail - just go through the normal ordering process using our shop and choose the 'Payment Details Sent Seperately' option, print out your order and send it to us by traditional mail.

Please make cheques and postal orders, in pounds Sterling only, payable to Y Ffynhonnell Ltd. Please note that if you pay by credit card, your credit card details will not be kept on file. We can accept Visa, Mastercard, Delta and Switch cards.

overseas orders
All transactions are processed in pounds sterling, so the exact cost of overseas orders will depend on exchange rates at the time your payment is processed.

browsers
Please note that the shop may not display or function properly in an old or unusual browser. It has been successfully tested in IE5, IE6, Netscape 4, Netscape 7 and Mozilla 5. If you have problems, please email us and let us know which browser (with version) you were using and what the error message was.

security
All credit and debit card payments are processed using SecureTrading’s secure processing system. Please read our security statement for more information. You will notice when you get to the Secure Trading checkout that the 'golden padlock' or 'golden key' appears in the bottom right hand corner of your browser - this indicates that you are on a secure server and that your credit card details are safe.

confirmation and delivery
Confirmation of your order will be sent automatically by email on receipt of your order. Please allow 28 days for the delivery.
