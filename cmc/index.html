welcome

Welcome to Clwb Malu Cachu, the website for Welsh learners. Here you will find grammar cheat sheets, audio files, short stories, interviews and lots, lots more!

If this is your first time here, then feel free to explore the site using the links on the left or at the bottom of each page. When you roll your mouse over the Welsh links, you’ll see the English translation to help you navigate through the site.


Stay in touch
To keep updated with additions to the CMC site, you can subscribe to our mailing list, join the CMC Yahoo Group discussion list, or visit our news blog. Staying in touch with CMC is easy!
